# Policy framework and guidelines on GenAI in education

### Framework with room for programme policy

The policy framework contains general and specific guidelines for the responsible use of GenAI in education. At the same time, it offers faculties and programmes room to translate the policy into their own educational practice. After all, GenAI requires customization: the impact and proper application differs per discipline.

### Getting started at programme level

Programmes are called upon to actively investigate what role GenAI can (start to) play within the curriculum, with a view to the changing labor market in a world with GenAI.  They are also encouraged to think about GenAI as a learning objective in itself: students should learn to use GenAI critically and responsibly.

### Assessment Tool

In addition to the policy framework, an addendum to the existing Assessment Policy Framework has been prepared. This addendum provides guidance for dealing with GenAI in assessments. It helps programmes to identify risks and possibilities and to adapt assessment if necessary. The basic principle is and remains that students must achieve demonstrable learning objectives independently.

### Policy Framework GenAI

This policy framework describes the UvA policy regarding Generative AI (GenAI) in education. It provides a central framework and guidelines for responsible use of GenAI in education based on scientific integrity. As AI develops, the UvA AI policy will be reviewed regularly.
